8 Days to Kyiv is a mostly linear text adventure about the plight of the Russian soldier. The characters are not real, but much of the story is based on actual events. I wove these experiences into a single narrative about a single soldier, borrowing heavily from the following videos:

> Text messages > Tow back to Russia > Sunflower seeds > Russian convoy traffic jam > Russian POW interview

Music:

> "Dark is the night" by Nikita Bogoslovsky (Main Menu) > "I Don't Want to Set the World on Fire" by The Ink Spots (Ending)
